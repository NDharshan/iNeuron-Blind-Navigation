About me in a few words

Srinivas

Of Brown frame, shoulder-length dark hair gently hangs over a round, tense face hidden in a cap. Lidded black eyes, set buried within their sockets, watch energetically over the families they've barely related to for so long. A gentle beard covering freckles spread elegantly across his cheeks and leaves a satisfying memory of his luck. This is the face of Srinivas, a true warrior among humans. He stands high among others, despite his scraggy frame. There's something seductive about him, perhaps it's a feeling of coldness or perhaps it's simply his sensitivity. But nonetheless, people tend to ask him to tell stories, while making up bigger stories about him.
